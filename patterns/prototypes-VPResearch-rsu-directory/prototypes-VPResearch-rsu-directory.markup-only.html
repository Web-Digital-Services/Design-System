RSU Directory

Animal Research Compliance Program (ARC)

Review and approve IACUC applications and provide required ongoing monitoring, Receive and review animal related prompt reporting (e.g. animal welfare concerns, non-compliance, unexpected adverse event), Assistance with IACUC related regulatory questions

Biggs Institute Biomarker Core

process biospecimens and run of neurodegenerative and inflammatory biomarker assays, using the Quanterix Simoa HD-X, Lumipulse G1200, MSD, and ELISAs., We also provide laboratory services such as protocol advice,, We also provide laboratory services such as biospecimen processing,, We also provide laboratory services such as freezer storage, We also provide laboratory services such as downstream sample processing and analysis., We have a repository consisting of <1200 participant samples that can be used to answer research questions.

Biggs Institute Imaging Core

3T MRI for clinical and research scanning, 2 scanners, PET scanning, advanced analytics using machine learning, imaging data processing, data sharing, we provide computational resources for advanced analytics

Biggs Institute San Antonio Heart Mind Study for Population Core: sensorimotor testing

Sensorimotor testing: 1) Motor (grip strength, gait, SPPB), Sensorimotor testing: 2) Smell test (B-SIT 12 item test), Sensorimotor testing: 3) Peripheral (Monofilament testing), Sensorimotor testing: 4) Hearing (audiometry, speech in noise), Sensorimotor testing: 5) Vestibular, Sensorimotor testing: 6) Retinal imaging in conjunction with Opthalmology

Biggs Institute San Antonio Heart Mind Study for Population Core: sensorimotor testing

study design, drafting a Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP), Lunch and Learn for Staff, data harmonization, preparing analysis datasets, statistical analysis, generation of tables and figures for manuscripts

Biggs Institute Statistical, Genetics & Multiomics Core:

Genetic testing,, Genomic Profiling,, Epigenomic Profiling, Biostatistics,, Bioinformatics

BioAnalytics and Single-Cell core (BASiC)

Mass Cytometry by Helios Immunoassay, Mass Cytometry by FlexMap3D Multiplex qRT-PCR, Mass Cytometry by BioMark HD, Single-cell manual isolation, NA

Biobehavioral Lab & Biobank

Biobanking and testing, Sample preparation for omic analysis (bulk or single cell) and data analysis, Customized antibody panels for comprehensive circulating human immune cell subtype profiling using CyTOF, Antibody subpanels related to stress, pain, depression, trauma, cancer hybrid cells and microenvironment profiling using CyTOF, Highly sensitive Meso Scale Discovery assays ELISA physical assessments, Body composition (i.e., physician scale, BMI assessment), Strength and fitness (i.e., baseline hydraulic hand dynamometer, ParvoMedic Metabolic Card), Cardiovascular function (i.e., pulse oximetry, blood pressure assessment)., Specimen collection (i.e., blood, urine, etc.), Diagnostic ordering (i.e., HbA1c, Comprehensive Metabolic Panel, etc.) neuropsychological testing, Wisconsin Card Sorting Test CANTAB (Cambridge Neuropsychological Test Automated Battery), D-KEFS (Delis-Kaplan Executive Function System)

Biospecimen and Translational Genomics Core Laboratory

Biospecimen Processing, Biospecimen Banking, Regulatory Document Preparation and Submission, Biospecimen Distribution, DNA/RNA Isolation, Gene Expression Analysis, Quantitative Real-Time PCR, Equipment Usage - Aperio Versa Digital Slide Scanning System, Equipment Usage - SPH1.108.6, Equipment Usage - BioRad CFX96, Equipment Usage - 384 Real Time PCR System, Equipment Usage MED5.570C; BioRad CX200 Droplet Digital PCR System - MED5.570C, Equipment Usage - BioRad CX200 Droplet Digital PCR System - MED5.570C

Biostatistics (Population Health Sciences)

study design, proposal development, Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP), statistical analysis, statistical reports, data science web applications, geocoding
