Directory of Research Resources

This page provides information about Research Administration offices, shared services, and resources available to faculty, staff, and trainees engaged in research at UT Health San Antonio. If you would like to suggest an additional resource for inclusion, please complete the research resource survey.


Animal Research Compliance Program (ARC)

Review and approve IACUC applications and provide required ongoing monitoring, Receive and review animal related prompt reporting (e.g. animal welfare concerns, non-compliance, unexpected adverse event), Assistance with IACUC related regulatory questions

Biggs Institute Biomarker Core

process biospecimens and run of neurodegenerative and inflammatory biomarker assays, using the Quanterix Simoa HD-X, Lumipulse G1200, MSD, and ELISAs., We also provide laboratory services such as protocol advice,, We also provide laboratory services such as biospecimen processing,, We also provide laboratory services such as freezer storage, We also provide laboratory services such as downstream sample processing and analysis., We have a repository consisting of <1200 participant samples that can be used to answer research questions.

Biggs Institute Imaging Core

3T MRI for clinical and research scanning, 2 scanners, PET scanning, advanced analytics using machine learning, imaging data processing, data sharing, we provide computational resources for advanced analytics

Biggs Institute San Antonio Heart Mind Study for Population Core: sensorimotor testing

Sensorimotor testing: 1) Motor (grip strength, gait, SPPB), Sensorimotor testing: 2) Smell test (B-SIT 12 item test), Sensorimotor testing: 3) Peripheral (Monofilament testing), Sensorimotor testing: 4) Hearing (audiometry, speech in noise), Sensorimotor testing: 5) Vestibular, Sensorimotor testing: 6) Retinal imaging in conjunction with Opthalmology

Biggs Institute San Antonio Heart Mind Study for Population Core: sensorimotor testing

study design, drafting a Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP), Lunch and Learn for Staff, data harmonization, preparing analysis datasets, statistical analysis, generation of tables and figures for manuscripts

Biggs Institute Statistical, Genetics & Multiomics Core:

Genetic testing,, Genomic Profiling,, Epigenomic Profiling, Biostatistics,, Bioinformatics

BioAnalytics and Single-Cell core (BASiC)

Mass Cytometry by Helios Immunoassay, Mass Cytometry by FlexMap3D Multiplex qRT-PCR, Mass Cytometry by BioMark HD, Single-cell manual isolation, NA

Biobehavioral Lab & Biobank

Biobanking and testing, Sample preparation for omic analysis (bulk or single cell) and data analysis, Customized antibody panels for comprehensive circulating human immune cell subtype profiling using CyTOF, Antibody subpanels related to stress, pain, depression, trauma, cancer hybrid cells and microenvironment profiling using CyTOF, Highly sensitive Meso Scale Discovery assays ELISA physical assessments, Body composition (i.e., physician scale, BMI assessment), Strength and fitness (i.e., baseline hydraulic hand dynamometer, ParvoMedic Metabolic Card), Cardiovascular function (i.e., pulse oximetry, blood pressure assessment)., Specimen collection (i.e., blood, urine, etc.), Diagnostic ordering (i.e., HbA1c, Comprehensive Metabolic Panel, etc.) neuropsychological testing, Wisconsin Card Sorting Test CANTAB (Cambridge Neuropsychological Test Automated Battery), D-KEFS (Delis-Kaplan Executive Function System)

Biospecimen and Translational Genomics Core Laboratory

Biospecimen Processing, Biospecimen Banking, Regulatory Document Preparation and Submission, Biospecimen Distribution, DNA/RNA Isolation, Gene Expression Analysis, Quantitative Real-Time PCR, Equipment Usage - Aperio Versa Digital Slide Scanning System, Equipment Usage - SPH1.108.6, Equipment Usage - BioRad CFX96, Equipment Usage - 384 Real Time PCR System, Equipment Usage MED5.570C; BioRad CX200 Droplet Digital PCR System - MED5.570C, Equipment Usage - BioRad CX200 Droplet Digital PCR System - MED5.570C

Biostatistics (Population Health Sciences)

study design, proposal development, Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP), statistical analysis, statistical reports, data science web applications, geocoding

Center for Biomedical Neuroscience

Scientific conferences - annual spring retreat, Scientific conferences - San Antonio Brain Health Symposium., Recruiting for post-doc and graduate student applicants at scientific conferences and through BrainBowl., Organization of multi-PI thematic Research Interest Groups, Pre-grant review and submission advising, specifically for foundation-defined limited submission application opportunities;, trainee professional development activities.

Center for Community-Based Health Promotion with Women and Children

Community based participatory research consultation

Center for Mitochondrial Medicine

Unit I: Mitochondrial Dysfunction in Humans Clinical Studies and Translational Research: Investigating the impact of mitochondrial dysfunction on diseases and aging., Unit I: Molecular and Cellular Characterization: Identifying the mechanisms underlying mitochondrial dysfunction, including mitochondrial DNA mutations and oxidative stress., Unit I: Development of Diagnostic and Therapeutic Approaches: Identifying novel biomarkers and therapeutic targets for mitochondrial diseases., Unit II: Molecular Mechanisms in Acute Stress and Chronic Disease Molecular and Cellular Research: Elucidating the mechanisms affecting organ function under stress and chronic conditions., Unit II: Therapeutic Strategy Development: Identifying drug targets and developing novel treatment approaches for diseases influenced by acute stress and chronic conditions., Unit III: Genome-Wide Screening for Intracellular Ion Dynamics Genomic and Proteomic Screening: Using advanced screening techniques to identify critical molecular components of ion dynamics., Unit III: Development of Methodologies: Creating new tools for studying ion dynamics, including imaging techniques and biosensors., Unit IV: Unbiased Biomarker Discovery in Human and Mouse Models Biomarker Research and Validation: Using genomics, proteomics, and metabolomics to discover and validate disease biomarkers., Unit IV: Assay and Platform Development: Translating biomarker discoveries into clinical practice with new diagnostic tools., Unit V: In Vitro and In Vivo Assays for Drug Screening Drug Screening Assays: Developing assays for medium and high-throughput screening of drug candidates., Unit V: Optimization of Drug Discovery Processes: Incorporating advanced technologies like microfluidics and 3D printing to enhance drug screening., Unit VI: Mechanism of Disease Pathogenesis and Therapeutic Strategies Model Development: Creating and utilizing disease models to understand pathogenesis and identify therapeutic interventions., Unit VI: Clinical Trial Preparation: Preparing and conducting preclinical and clinical trials to test new treatments., Unit VI: Cross-Unit Services Educational Programs and Training: Offering rigorous research training and mentorship for graduate students and postdoctoral fellows., Unit VI: Lecture Series and Seminars: Organizing and hosting events with guest lecturers to foster education and collaboration in mitochondrial medicine., Unit VI: Special Initiatives Collaboration and Partnerships: Fostering interdisciplinary collaborations and establishing partnerships with other research entities.

Center for Mucosal & Microbiology (CMMB)

microbiome research etc, multiomic approaches, microbiome research etc

Center for Precision Medicine

Spatial metabolomics on tissue sections, Targeted metabolomics on tissue sections, Targeted metabolomics on urine, Targeted metabolomics on blood
