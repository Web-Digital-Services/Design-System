Overview

The Center for Hispanic Healthy Aging (CHHA) builds on UT Health San Antonio strengths in Hispanic aging, and population based longitudinal studies in aging with a wide socio-economic perspective. The Center seeks to foster communication and collaboration between investigators and scholars with a shared interest in population aging with a life-course perspective with an emphasis in past migration, childhood conditions, and socio-economic and health disparities.

UT Health San Antonio has an outstanding group of investigators funded by NIH and other agencies in the areas of Hispanic aging, physical and cognitive function, lifestyle and behavioral patterns, with deep interest in social determinants of health.
