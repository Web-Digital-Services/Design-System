Our array of innovative, science-backed programming empowers communities and individuals through innovative programs.

Be Well Clinic

Providing in-person and virtual care, our clinic delivers compassionate treatment for substance use and mental health needs, including specialized adolescent services. Ask about no- and low-cost options.

Center for Substance Use Training & Telementoring

C-STAT offers evidence-based training, mentorship, and technical assistance for healthcare professionals to enhance their skills in addressing substance use disorders, utilizing the latest evidence-based practices.

Naloxone Texas

Naloxone Texas provides free opioid overdose reversal medication and prevention training to individuals and organizations. Increasing access to life-saving medication strengthens community safety and supports those affected by substance use.

Be Well Texas Provider Network

This statewide collaboration of health care providers offering treatment, recovery, and hospital-based services empowers local solutions to local challenges and ensures comprehensive care across Texas.

Research & Clinical Trials

Our leading-edge research unit drives innovation in substance use care, from clinical trials to implementation science studies that inform better health policies and practices.

Health Communications

The Health Communications team develops innovative, stigma-reducing campaigns and educational initiatives to connect Texans with accurate, accessible information about substance use treatment and recovery.
