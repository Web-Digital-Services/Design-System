UT Health SA EpicCare Link is a web-based portal offered to our community partners and affiliates.

UT Health SA EpicCare Link provides secure access to our community partners to select and view patient information.

Utilization of UT Health SA EpicCare Link comes at no cost to our community partners and affiliates.

Benefits of EpicCare Link include:

  • Easily Accessible
  • Multi-functional
  • Physician to Physician Messaging
  • Order transparency
  • Real-Time Patient Updated Information
