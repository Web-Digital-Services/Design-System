Association of Retired Faculty and Associates

A great way for retired faculty, administrative & professional personnel and classified staff of the UT Health family to remain connected.

  • Home
  • Giving
  • Membership
  • Contact Us

The Association of Retired Faculty & Associates (ARFA) of UT Health San Antonio is a great way for retired faculty, administrative & professional personnel and classified staff of the UT Health family to remain connected. Spouses are also welcome to join. Members enjoy luncheons, campus events, and an annual holiday party.

In addition to serving as a connection point, ARFA supports a scholarship award to a deserving student each year. Supporting these scholarship awards is our way of investing in the potential of our students and the impact that they will have in their future careers.

For more information about ARFA, contact Matt Scott scottm5@uthscsa.edu or 210-567-2003.

If you have any questions about UT Health Benefits, contact ben-admin@uthscsa.edu or 210-567-8847.
