About Us

CPAN, or the Child Psychiatry Access Network is Texas pediatric clinicians' free and trusted mental health resource. UT Health San Antonio serves as an access center providing child and adolescent behavioral health consultation services and training opportunities for pediatric primary care providers.

Primary care providers may call the CPAN hotline at 1-(888)-901-2726 Select Option 3, then 2 to consult with a psychologist or psychiatrist within 30 minutes of their call.

Primary care providers and pediatricians alike may ask about mental health assessment, symptoms and psychiatric medications and gain referrals for mental health providers in the community with their patients and families.

How Does CPAN Work?

It's simple to enroll and easy to use. CPAN staff are available Monday-Friday, 8am-5pm. No call is too small. We are here to support you.

  • 1

    Call 888-901-2726 Press 3 for Valley and Central regions, then press 2 for UT Health San Antonio

  • 2

    Provide basic information to a behavioral health specialist

  • 3

    A UT Health San Antonio Psychiatrist will call you back within 30 minutes

    • Consult with a board-certified child and adolescent psychiatrist
    • Discuss diagnosis, treatment, medication options and more
    • Get resources for behavioral health providers, based on patient location and insurance
